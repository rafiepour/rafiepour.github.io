In general, I think NLP is at the forefront of science and will reshape how we live our lives in the near future. This change should be for the good and be focused on improving efficiency in organizations and promote well-informed decision making. 

Prior to graduate school and alongside my undergraduate studies, I spent five years as an entrepreneur, programming mobile apps & backend services for the Iranian local market. My background in both the industry and academia gives me a unique perspective on their challenges and benefits. I'm choosing to stay in academia in the future because I feel there is no point in discovering new knowledge if you're the only one benefiting from it.
